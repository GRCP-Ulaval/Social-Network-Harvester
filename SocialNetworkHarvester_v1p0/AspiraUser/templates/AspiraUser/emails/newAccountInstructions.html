In any case, the webmaster will contact you as soon as your account has been activated (generally under 24h) or, if he/she has any questions regarding your intentions or your organization.

In the meantime, note that the SNH works using the public APIs of Twitter, Facebook, Youtube and other social medias. This means that, in order to respect the Terms and Conditions of each medium, the data collected using the SNH must be used only as a mean to explore insights and generate pretty graphs. If published in any form, the data MUST be anonymized by removing any piece of information wich can be used to identify someone.

Finally, in order to use this service, you will need a specialized "app" for each social media you wish to "harvest". An app consists of a set of authentification informations which can be used to communicate programmatically with an API. In order to create one of these, please use the appropriate link and follow the official instructions.

Twitter: apps.twitter.com
Facebook: developers.facebook.com
Youtube developers.google.com

As soon as your SNH account is validated, you will be able to access your account settings page and enter the relevant app informations. Note that no (new) data will be harvested according to your requests until your account is properly configured with the corresponding apps.

Finally, thank you again for using the Social Network Harvester! Remember to use your new powers responsibly!
